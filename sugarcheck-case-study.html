The Problem

Diabetes is a growing epidemic in Australia, with approximately 6% of Australian adults suffering from either Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes. Ageing adults are especially at risk, and its estimated that around 1 in 6 people aged over 65 are diagnosed with the disease.

Managing diabetes in older people poses its own unique challenges, and many are unable to take the appropriate medication needed due to the potential complications with other age-related diseases. As such, many ageing adults need to rely on alternative methods to keep their diabetes in check, otherwise they run the risk of developing more chronic conditions in the long-term.
